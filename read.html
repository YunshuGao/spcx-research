0. 一句话总结 / Bottom line

SpaceX 已于 2026 年 6 月 12 日以代码 SPCX 在纳斯达克上市，发行价 135 美元，首日收 161 美元（+19%）。「打新」窗口已关闭——你现在只能在二级市场以约 161 美元买入。估值极高（约 95 倍市销率），公司整体仍亏损。它已不是「纯火箭公司」，而是「火箭 + 星链 + xAI(Grok) + X(推特)」综合体。可以买，但请小仓位、分批、用限价单。

SpaceX listed on Nasdaq as SPCX on 12 Jun 2026 at US$135, closing day one at US$161 (+19%). The subscription window is closed — you can now only buy on the secondary market at ~US$161. It's priced extremely high (~95× sales) and is still loss-making overall. It's no longer a pure rocket company — it's rockets + Starlink + xAI (Grok) + X (Twitter). You can buy it, but keep the position small, stagger entries, and use limit orders.

1. 时间点：你已经错过打新 / Timing: the subscription window has closed

  • 招股书（prospectus / S-1）5 月 20 日公开；6 月 11 日定价 135 美元；6 月 12 日上市收 161 美元。
  • 澳洲的官方零售打新券商是 CommSec，但申请已于 6 月 10 日截止。
  • 你拿不到 135 美元打新价了。现在买等于在首日 +19% 的高点、上市第二天进场——新股波动最剧烈的阶段。
  • The prospectus (S-1) went public 20 May; priced at $135 on 11 Jun; listed 12 Jun, closed $161.
  • In Australia, CommSec was the official retail IPO broker, but applications closed 10 Jun.
  • You can no longer get the $135 IPO price. Buying now means a 19% premium on day two — the most volatile phase for a hot IPO.

小知识：打新 (IPO subscription) = 上市前以发行价申购；二级市场 (secondary market) = 上市后在交易所自由买卖。你现在只能走二级市场。

Insight: IPO subscription = buying at the offer price before listing; secondary market = trading freely on the exchange after listing. You can only use the secondary market now.

2. 你买的到底是什么？/ What are you actually buying?

多数人以为「SpaceX = 火箭」，其实不对。通过一连串「同一控制人」并购：xAI 在 2025 年 3 月收购了 X（推特），SpaceX 又在 2026 年 2 月收购了 xAI。招股书把三者合并报表。所以一股 SPCX = 火箭（Falcon/Starship/Dragon）+ 星链 + xAI/Grok + X 平台。你买的核心赌注其实是 AI，而不只是火箭。

  • Space 航天：火箭与飞船，全球 80%+ 入轨质量、99%+ 成功率。
  • Connectivity 连接（星链）：约 9,600 颗卫星、1,030 万订阅、164 国——赚钱引擎。
  • AI（xAI/X）：Grok、COLOSSUS 超算、X（约 5.5 亿月活）——烧钱黑洞。

Most people think "SpaceX = rockets." Not quite. Through common-control deals — xAI bought X (Twitter) in Mar 2025, then SpaceX bought xAI in Feb 2026 — the prospectus combines all three. So one SPCX share = rockets (Falcon/Starship/Dragon) + Starlink + xAI/Grok + the X platform. Your core bet is really AI, not just rockets.

  • Space: rockets & spacecraft; ~80%+ of global mass-to-orbit, 99%+ success.
  • Connectivity (Starlink): ~9,600 satellites, 10.3M subscribers, 164 countries — the profit engine.
  • AI (xAI/X): Grok, COLOSSUS superclusters, X (~550M MAUs) — the cash incinerator.

4. 估值：到底贵不贵？/ Valuation: how expensive?

  • 总股本约 130.8 亿股；发行价市值约 1.77 万亿，首日收盘约 2.1 万亿——史上最大 IPO。
  • 市销率 P/S ≈ 95 倍（发行价）/ ≈113 倍（收盘）。对比：最优质盈利软件股 15–25 倍，英伟达最热 ~30–40 倍。一家仍亏损的综合体给到 ~95–113 倍，是「风险投资式」定价。
  • 摊薄：你付 135 美元，但每股有形账面价值仅 7.85 美元，每股 127.15 美元是溢价。
  • 扎心对比：现有股东（含马斯克）平均成本 6.48 美元/股；你付 135 美元。新股东出了历史全部投入资金的 48%（750 亿），却只拿 4.2% 股权。
  • ~13.08B shares; ≈$1.77T at IPO, ≈$2.1T at the day-one close — the largest IPO ever.
  • P/S ≈ 95× (IPO) / ≈113× (close). For context: premium profitable software 15–25×, Nvidia at its frothiest ~30–40×. ~95–113× for a loss-making conglomerate is venture-style pricing.
  • Dilution: you pay $135 but net tangible book value is only $7.85/share — a $127.15/share premium.
  • Stark comparison: existing holders (incl. Musk) averaged $6.48/share; you pay $135. New investors put in 48% of all capital ever ($75B) for just 4.2% of the company.

小知识：市销率 P/S = 市值 ÷ 年收入，用来给尚未盈利的高增长公司估值。倍数越高，市场对未来增长预期越极端、容错率越低。

Insight: P/S = market cap ÷ annual revenue, used to value not-yet-profitable growth companies. The higher the multiple, the more flawless future growth is priced in — and the less room for error.

5. 股权与控制权 / Share structure & control

  • 双层股权：A 类 1 票/股（你买的）；B 类 10 票/股（马斯克的）。
  • 上市后马斯克掌握约 82.4% 投票权（仅持约 46% 经济股权）。A 类总投票权 11.5%，B 类 88.5%。
  • SpaceX 是「受控公司」，可豁免「董事会多数独立董事」等治理要求。
  • 章程放弃公司机会原则：马斯克与董事可做与公司竞争的生意；还有强制仲裁/指定法院条款，限制你起诉的能力。
  • 关键人物风险：马斯克同时是特斯拉 CEO，还有 Neuralink、Boring；招股书明说他不全职、且公司没买关键人物保险。
  • 彩蛋：马斯克部分激励股权要「在火星建成至少 100 万人永久殖民地」「太空 100 太瓦/年算力」才解锁——招股书原文。
  • Dual-class: Class A = 1 vote/share (yours); Class B = 10 votes/share (Musk's).
  • Post-IPO Musk controls ~82.4% of votes (with ~46% of the economic equity). Class A = 11.5% of votes, Class B = 88.5%.
  • SpaceX is a "controlled company" — exempt from rules like a majority-independent board.
  • The charter renounces the corporate-opportunity doctrine: Musk and directors may pursue competing ventures; mandatory arbitration / exclusive-forum clauses limit your ability to sue.
  • Key-man risk: Musk is also Tesla CEO, plus Neuralink and Boring; the S-1 says he is not full-time and there is no key-person insurance on him.
  • Easter egg: part of Musk's incentive shares vest only on "a permanent human colony on Mars with ≥1 million inhabitants" and "100 TW/yr of orbital compute" — genuinely in the prospectus.

小知识：双层股权让创始人用少数经济股权掌握多数投票权。好处是能长期投入、不受短期股东干扰；坏处是你几乎没有发言权，治理完全系于一人。

Insight: Dual-class lets a founder keep voting control with minority ownership. Upside: long-term focus. Downside: you have almost no say; governance rests on one person.

6. 禁售期与抛压 / Lock-up & supply overhang

现在流通盘很小（约 5.56 亿 A 股），容易被炒高。但未来 12 个月被锁定的股票会分批解禁，供给可能膨胀 20 倍以上，形成结构性卖压：

  • 基础禁售期 180 天，但有多个提前解禁点；
  • 价格触发：若首份财报前 10 个交易日内有 5 天收盘 ≥ 发行价 +30%（≥175.50 美元），提前多解锁约 10%；
  • 二季度财报后解锁约 20%；70/90/105/120/135 天各 7%；三季度财报后一次性约 28%（约 13 亿股）；
  • 约 60%（约 78 亿股）延长禁售至 366 天，其中马斯克约 64 亿股在第 366 天解禁，且他不参与提前解禁。

The float is tiny now (~556M Class A), so it's easy to bid up. But over the next 12 months locked shares release in tranches, potentially expanding supply 20×+ — a structural selling pressure:

  • Base 180-day lock-up, but with several early-release triggers;
  • Price trigger: if it closes ≥30% above the IPO price (≥$175.50) on 5 of 10 days into the first earnings date, ~10% unlocks early;
  • ~20% after Q2 earnings; 7% at days 70/90/105/120/135; a big ~28% (~1.3B shares) after Q3 earnings;
  • ~60% (~7.8B shares) on an extended 366-day lock, incl. Musk's ~6.4B at day 366 — he is excluded from early releases.

小知识：禁售期 (lock-up) = 内部人 IPO 后一段时间内不得卖股的承诺。解禁日常带来抛压、压制股价——与基本面无关。这是首日大涨后常见的回落原因。

Insight: A lock-up is a promise by insiders not to sell for a period after IPO. Expiry dates bring selling pressure regardless of fundamentals — a common reason hot IPOs fade after the day-one pop.

  • ~95× sales, loss-making (−$4.9B); AI burning $12.7B capex; Starlink ARPU falling ($99→$91→$81); Musk 82.4% votes, part-time, no key-man insurance; a 12-month unlock flood; Jim Chanos calls it "hopes and dreams."

11. 防骗提醒 / Scam warning ⚠️

现在能合法持有 SpaceX 的唯一方式，是通过正规券商买上市代码 SPCX。任何通过邮件、Telegram、WhatsApp、微信群发给你的「打折 pre-IPO SpaceX 内部额度 / 保证回报 / SPV 份额」都是骗局。美国 SEC 多次就「假冒 SpaceX pre-IPO」诈骗发警告；有人通过 SPV 买的「SpaceX」上市后才发现根本没有可交易股票。请确认券商持有 ASIC AFSL 牌照。

The only legitimate way to own SpaceX now is buying the listed SPCX ticker through a regulated broker. Any "discounted pre-IPO SpaceX allocation / guaranteed returns / SPV interest" via email, Telegram, WhatsApp or WeChat is a scam. The SEC has repeatedly warned about fake "pre-IPO SpaceX" fraud; some SPV "owners" find they hold nothing tradable. Verify your broker holds an ASIC AFSL.
